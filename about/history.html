Academic System & Intakes

Overview of our programs, semesters, and examination schedule

Market and Skill-Driven Programs

We offer market and skill driven diploma and certificate courses.

Semester System and Intakes

We operate a semester system and we have two semesters a year with two intakes; the March and August intakes with both full time and weekend students.

Examinations & Center Number

Students under these intakes sit their exams every Nov / Dec and May / June as per the UBTEB and DIT calendar every year under centre number UBTEB172.
