Vice Chancellor, Prof. Openjuru L. George on why Gulu University and not any other...

Anybody interested in rural Development or community transformation should come to Gulu University (GU). This call is based on the fact that Gulu University is an Agriculturally focused University, and all our programs are geared towards the promotion of Agri-activity for the rural community.

This focus includes Medicine, where we train for rural-based health facilities; in Education, we train teachers for rural schools and Agricultural Education.
